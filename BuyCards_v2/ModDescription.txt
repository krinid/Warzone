Makes cards purchasable with gold. Requires Commerce mode to be enabled. Supports both Regular cards (standard cards built into Warzone) and Custom cards defined by mods.

Options:
• Max # of purchases: The limit is shared among all players. When a given card type reaches the limit, no further cards of that type can be purchased by any player. Set to -1 for unlimited card purchases.
• Card price increase: Card price for a given card type increases on the following turn by a fixed % of original base card price each turn a card purchase of that type is made by any player. # of cards purchased and # of players purchasing the card does not change the increase amount.
