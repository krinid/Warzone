Throw poison on a territory that lasts for some number of turns. Poison can cause fixed and % damage to both armies and Special Units.

For Special Units with Health, their Health will be reduced by the appropriate amount.

For Special Units of 'Damage to Kill' type (no Health, they must be killed in a single attack, eg: Commanders), the 'Damage to Kill' value will be reduced.

If the Health or Damage to Kill values of a Special Units reaches 0, it will die.

Armies or Special Units impacted by poison can leave the territory in order to end the effects.
